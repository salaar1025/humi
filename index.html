My Dearest Humiaa,

Happy 27th Birthday 🌸

It's honestly a little strange how someone you met just a few months ago can start to feel so comfortable, so familiar, but that's exactly what you became to me. From our very first video call, there was this ease, this calmness in talking to you that I didn't expect… but I'm really grateful for it.

We've never had a fight, not even a moment where things felt heavy between us, and that says a lot. In such a short time, you gave me a space that felt peaceful, safe, and real… and that's rare.

I know life hasn't been easy on you, especially where you are right now in Malaysia. You've been dealing with so much, probably more than you even show, but even then, you carry yourself with such strength and softness at the same time. And honestly… I admire that more than I can explain.

On your birthday, I don't just wish you success or achievements, I wish you peace. The kind of peace that stays. I wish that whatever is heavy in your heart becomes lighter, that your problems slowly fade, and that life finally starts being gentle with you.

May Allah protect you wherever you are, guide you through every difficulty, and replace every worry of yours with something beautiful. May He give you strength when you feel tired, comfort when you feel alone, and happiness that reaches you in unexpected ways. And if there's any pain written for you, may He make it easier… or take it away from you completely, Ameen.

And I won't lie… in this short time, you've become someone I genuinely like, not just for how you talk, but for who you are. There's something about you that feels rare… and real.

Thank you for being you. Truly.

I hope today brings you even a small moment where you forget all your worries and just smile, because you deserve that, more than you think.

With all my heart,
Your friend from Pakistan 🇵🇰
